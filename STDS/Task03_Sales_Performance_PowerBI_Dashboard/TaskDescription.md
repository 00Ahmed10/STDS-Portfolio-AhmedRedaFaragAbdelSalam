# Sales Performance Power BI Dashboard

## Task Description
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales performance, revenue, profit,
and customer behavior across channels, categories, and time.

The goal of this dashboard is to monitor sales performance, profitability, 
and customer behavior to support data-driven business decisions.

## Business Questions Answered
- Which sales channel generates higher revenue and profit?
- How does revenue and profit change monthly?
- Which product categories contribute most to revenue?
- How strong is the profit margin over time?
- What is the customer return rate?

## Key Insights
- Revenue is almost evenly split between Direct and Wholesale channels.
- Profit margin remains strong at 35%.
- Dresses generate the highest revenue among categories.
- Sales peak during specific months, showing seasonality.
- Return rate of 26% highlights an opportunity for customer retention improvement.

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel (optional for source data)
